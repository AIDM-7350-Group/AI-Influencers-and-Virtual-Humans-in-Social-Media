DIGITAL PROPHECY

FUTURE DIRECTIONS

A glimpse into the evolution of virtual humans and AI-powered digital personalities.

EVOLUTION TIMELINE

Based on recent literature (2023-2025) and industry shifts.

INTERACTIVITY

🤖 Higher Interactivity via AI

Virtual influencers will move beyond static posts. With Generative AI, they will interact with fans in real-time. Future research focuses on how this interaction makes virtual entities feel like genuine social partners (Audrezet, 2025).

STRATEGY

💰 Shift to Brand-Owned Assets

Brands are reducing reliance on external virtual influencers. Creating in-house digital ambassadors allows for 100% risk control, avoiding human scandals and building unique, long-term brand equity.

GOVERNANCE

🛡️ Ethics & Transparency

As "deepfakes" concerns grow, transparency will be the core metric for trust. Clear labels and legal frameworks will eliminate consumer deception and build a healthy industry (Khalfallah & Keller, 2025).

PSYCHOLOGY

❤️ Emotional Attachment

Research is shifting from "visual realism" to "psychological connection." The trend is to study parasocial relationships and cultural differences in emotional responses (Bang, 2025; Pujadas-Gómez et al., 2025).

AUTONOMY

🧠 AI-Generated Personalities

Future virtual influencers will have greater autonomy, developing their own opinions and behavioral patterns based on big data feedback. They can build highly recognizable personal brands on social media 24/7.
